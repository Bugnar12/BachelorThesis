Privacy Policy

Gmail Phishing Detector is a Chrome extension that analyzes Gmail emails for potential phishing threats. We take your privacy seriously.

What Data Is Collected

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. It only analyzes the content of the currently opened Gmail email in your browser tab, and sends necessary content (like email body and URLs) to a secure backend server for phishing detection.

Use of Data

The data analyzed is used solely to detect phishing attempts. No data is stored permanently. The extension does not access any data outside of Gmail unless explicitly authorized by you.

Third-party Services

This extension communicates with a backend API hosted at bachelorthesis-production-8acf.up.railway.app for phishing detection. No data is shared with advertisers or analytics providers.

Permissions

The extension requests access to Gmail tabs and limited browser APIs strictly for functionality. No unnecessary permissions are used.
